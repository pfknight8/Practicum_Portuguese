# Conjuncitons and Interrogatives

Conjunção e Interrogativas

This file will cover the vocabulary of Conjunctions and Interrogatives, since both are short lists.

Interrogative words in English can function as many different parts of speech, conjunctions being one of them. Since there is a short list of conjunctions, and many can serve this function, I've included them here together.


## Interrogatives

In this section, we will explore words and phrases that are used for forming questions.

### *List of interrogatives*

| English  | Português    |
|:---------|:-------------|
| who      | quem         |

| when     | quando       |
| where    | onde         |
| why      | por quê      |
| how      | como         |
| which    | qual         |
| how many | quantos      |
| how much | quanto custa |
